Steam locomotive construction and restoration

Construction of new steam engines, along with restoration of old ones, would bring these benefits:

  1. Stem the flow of young people out of the region in search of Melbourne and interstate jobs
  2. Potentially create a niche steam locomotive export industry.
  3. Make the goldfields a center for steam locomotive restoration and maintenance.
  4. Provide a wide range of apprenticeships.
  5. Preserve dying trades.

Wider benefits

Wider benefits for the goldfields community would include:

  1. Provide much need jobs of old and young alike.
  2. Stem the flow of young people out of the region in search of Melbourne and interstate jobs
  3. Give the goldfields back some pride in their towns and their region.
  4. Generate funds to restore historic building across the region.
  5. Raise living standards.

Youth

The regions youth could be encouraged to be active as tour guides, or to assist with guided tours. Particularly bicycle and horseback tours around the goldfields or leading tourist groups along the rail trail between Maryborough and Castlemaine.

  1. They would gain valualbe customer service skils and self esteem.
  2. They would gain a sense of hope for a propserous future for themselves and be more likely to stay out of trouble.
  3. They would have less incentive to leave for jobs in the city or interstate.

  1. Allow indigenous Australians could show off their bush knowledge and survival prowess.
