Steam locomotive construction:

  1. Stem the flow of young people out of the region in search of Melbourne and interstate jobs
  2. Potentially create a niche steam locomotive export industry.
  3. Make the goldfields a center for steam locomotive restoration and maintenance.
  4. Provide a wide range of apprenticeships.
  5. Preserve dying trades.

Wider benefits

  1. Provide much need jobs of old and young alike.
  2. Stem the flow of young people out of the region in search of Melbourne and interstate jobs
  3. Give the goldfields back some pride in their towns and their region.
  4. Generate funds to restore historic building across the region.
  5. Raise living standards.
