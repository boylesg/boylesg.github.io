Trees

Allocasurina littoralis / Black Sheoak

Acacia pycnantha / Golden Wattle

Banksia marginata / Silver Banksia

These are small to medium trees.

The flowers are rich in nectar and attract a wide variety of native birds.

Banksia praemorsa / Cut Leaf Banksia

These are small to medium trees.

The flowers are rich in nectar and attact a wide variety of native birds.

There are varities with both lemon yellow and burdundy colored flowers.

Brachychiton_acerifolius / Flame Tree

In Victoria these grow as small to medium trees.

They are deciduous over summer.

They put on a spectacular display from spring to summer.

Brachychiiton rupestris / Bottle Tree

This is a small tree in Victoria.

The trunk swells hugely when the ground is wet and contracts when it is dry, so this is not a regular woody tree.

It is in fact Australia's version of a succulent plant.

It is a striking feature tree for small gardens.

Corymbia ficifolia / Flowering Gum

In Victoria these grow as small to medium trees.

Eucalyptus pauciflora / Snow Gum

Outside the alpine areas these grow as small to medium trees.

Hymenosporum_flavum / Native Frangipani

In Victoria these grow as small trees.

The flowers have a similar fragrance to exotic fragipanis.
