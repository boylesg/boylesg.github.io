Click here to learn why Millhouse is a worthy cause...

The Central Goldfields region has experienced significant economic decline since the 1990s, largely as a result of globalisation. This has led to the closure or downsizing of many major employers in the area, resulting in substantially reduced employment opportunities for residents and widespread financial hardship. Young people within the Central Goldfields community have been particularly affected by these circumstances.

Millhouse is a not-for-profit community organisation, governed and operated by volunteers from the Maryborough community. It provides opportunities for both older and younger residents of the Central Goldfields region to socialise and participate in a wide range of activities, including knitting, painting, scrap booking and Dungeons & Dragons. Millhouse also offers access to meals and coffee, employment assistance, no-interest loan support, mental health assistance, and basic groceries, all at little or no cost.

A significant proportion of Central Goldfields residents are unable to afford participation in comparable activities offered by commercial venues and would otherwise face continued social isolation at home. Millhouse therefore serves as an essential support system for many individuals in the region. It represents a highly worthwhile and impactful cause for potential donors to support.
