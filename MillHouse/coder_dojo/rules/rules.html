Photos FAQ Rules Information

For participants

  • Maximum age for students is 17
  • Students must be accompanied by one parent or guardian
  • Each student must bring their own laptop
  • Any Dojo equipment cannot be taken home
  • Any electronics components for personal projects must be purchased by the parent of the student
  • If participants miss 4 sessions in a row then they forfeit their place to the next participants on the waiting list

For volunteer teachers

  • Knowledge of coding is an advantage however it is not a requirement to be a volunteer teacher.
  • All volunteers must have a Working With Children Check
  • Volunteers must be 18 or older.
