Q: What does it cost to join?
A: It's free to join.

Q: How do I get my child involved?
A: Just bring them along to a session and register on the day.

Q: Is there a limit on numbers?
A: Yes. There is a limit if 20 children.

Q: The year is underway, is it too late to join?
A: It’s never too late to join.

Q: When are your sessions?
A: Every first Saturday of the month at 11:00am.

Q: Where are the sessions held?
A: Epping Community Center, 378 Findon Road, Epping, 3076.

Q: Can Ieave my child there and go shopping or attend appointments?
A: No. Each child under the age of 16 requires a parent to attend with them.

The reasons for this are as follow:

  • Parents need to manage the behaviour of their own child thus leaving volunteer teachers free to teach and mentor.
  • Volunteer teachers are not necessarily university trained teachers and they do not want to be left in the position of having to discipline your child.
  • Parents can learn along side their child and therefore be of some assistance to their child outside session times
  • It is a great opportunity for parents to bond with their child.

Q: What ages can join?
A: 7 - 17.

Q: Do I need a laptop for my child?
A: Yes.

Q: Can you supply a laptop?
A: No, not at this stage.

Q: Can you recommend a laptop?
A: Any laptop capable of running windows 10 or Windows 11, and with some USB A ports will be suitable. You do not need a high end laptop.

Q: Can I help?
A: Yes you can volunteer as a teacher but you will need to apply for a Working With Children Check. A volunteer check will not cost you anything.
