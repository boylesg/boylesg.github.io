Learning coding in this club is project based. There are no lectures, no text books, no work sheets and no tests. You will learn by doing, much like  apprentice electricians and plumbers do.

The goal is to be able to implement any project of your choice with minimal help. This might be an entire interactive web site, a wheeled obstacle avoiding robot or a game. However it will take time and lots of practice outside session times to gain the knowledge and confidence to do this. Likely around 12 months, and depending on how much effort you put in outside session times.

Coding and digital electronics is like soccer, football and basketball. Only those with passion for the game, hunger to learn and self discipline to practice will ever make it to the top leagues. Coding and digital electronics are no different.
