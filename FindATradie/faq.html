  • Q. How much will it cost me to join?
  • A. If you are a tradie then it will cost you $200 per year.
        If you are a customer then it will cost you $0 per year.

  • Q. Do I have to pay for leads?
  • A. You do not have to pay an additional fee for leads.

  • Q. How much will it cost me to list a job for tradies?
  • A. It will cost you $2 for each job you list.

  • Q. Do your store bank account and credit card numbers?
  • A. No. All payments are made through Paypal, who a responsible for the security of those numbers.

  • Q. Why do I have to join to find a tradie?
  • A. Because we want this system to work be fair for both tradies and their customers.

        If a customer pays a tradie promptly for a completed job then that tradie can leave that customer a positive rating.
        If a customer failes pays a tradie promptly for a completed job, or refuses to pay, then that tradie can leave that customer a negative rating.
        If a tradie completes job on time and on or close to budget then that customer can leave the tradie a positive rating.
        If a tradie fails to complete a job on time or close to budget then that customer can leave the tradie a negative rating.
        We don't want to allow customers to post frivalous jobs for tradies without consequences.
        All ratings are editable at any time.
        So parties in disagreement are free to negotiate and change negative ratings if an agreement is struck.
        We accept no responsibility for any negative feedback received by any party.

  • Q. Does findatradie provide guarentees on workmanship or refunds?
  • A. No. It is simply a platform to allow tradies to connect with customers or potential customers. We play no role in business relationships between tradies and their customers.
