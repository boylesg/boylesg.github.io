Stated purpose

  • Advocacy for Reopening: The group acts to ensure the decommissioned heritage pool is rehabilitated and reopened, rather than being forgotten or left in disrepair.
  • Funding Advocacy & Partnerships: FOMOP has worked with the Central Goldfields Shire Council to help secure over $9 million in state and federal funding for the project.
  • Community Mobilization: The group acts as a representative voice for the community, organizing events like town-wide garage sales to raise awareness, build momentum, and fundraise for the pool's rejuvenation.
  • "Save Our Swimming Pool" Effort: They operate as a collective, encouraging community-wide participation to engage with the council and ensure the project remains a high priority.
